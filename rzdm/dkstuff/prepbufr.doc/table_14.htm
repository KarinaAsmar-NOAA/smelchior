Table 14.   Reason codes associated with step "VIRTMP", program code 008 (last revised 1/4/2008).
 

NOTE:
      See the document "Summary of Virtual Temperature Processing in PREPBUFR" for more information.

KEY:
      IN "R.CODE" COLUMN:
                                            B - OCCURS IN ALL OPERATIONAL VERSIONS OF VIRTMP EXCEPT RUC (THE VIRTMP STEP DOES NOT RUN IN THE RUC NETWORK).

      IN "MNEMONIC" COLUMN:
                                            # - NEW VALUE CALCULATED.
 
R.CODE MEANING MNEMONICS AFFECTED AND NEW VALUE FOR OBS, Q.M.
00 B SPECIFIC HUMIDITY OBSERVATION IS REGENERATED FROM REPORTED DEWPOINT TEMPERATURE AND PRESSURE. (DEWPOINT TEMPERATURE HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE CQC AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS). THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. QRC-> QOB=#
00 B  TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 ABOVE OR 01 BELOW) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. TRC-> TOB=#
01 B SPECIFIC HUMIDITY OBSERVATION IS REGENERATED FROM REPORTED DEWPOINT TEMPERATURE AND PRESSURE. (DEWPOINT TEMPERATURE HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE CQC AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS). THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE PROGRAM PREPOBS_CQCBUFR. QRC-> QOB=#
01 B TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 OR 01 ABOVE) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE PROGRAM PREPOBS_CQCBUFR. TRC-> TOB=#
02 B TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 OR 01 ABOVE) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS.  EITHER THE SPECIFIC HUMIDITY HAS A REJECTED QUALITY MARKER OTHER THAN 9 OR 15 - OR - THE SPECIFIC HUMIDITY HAS A REJECTED QUALITY MARKER OF 9 OR 15 AND THE SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE QUALITY MARKER IS REJECTED BUT WITH A VALUE OTHER THAN 9 OR 15.  VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE IS CONSIDERED REJECTED.   THIS IS VALID ONLY FOR SURFACE LAND OR MARINE DATA TYPES. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. TRC-> TOB=#, TQM=8
03 B REPORTED TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS ALREADY VIRTUAL. NO CONVERSION IS NECESSARY. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. TRC-> no values changed
04 B TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 OR 01 ABOVE) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS.  THE SPECIFIC HUMIDITY HAS A REJECTED QUALITY MARKER OF 9 OR 15 AND THE  SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE QUALITY MARKER IS EITHER NOT REJECTED OR IS REJECTED BUT WITH A VALUE OF 9 OR 15. VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE IS CONSIDERED REJECTED.  THIS IS VALID ONLY FOR SURFACE LAND OR MARINE DATA TYPES. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. TRC-> TOB=#, TQM=9 (see % below)
06 B TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 OR 01 ABOVE) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS.  THE SPECIFIC HUMIDITY HAS A REJECTED QUALITY MARKER OTHER THAN 9 OR 15, THE PRESSURE LEVEL IS BELOW 700 MB, AND THE SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE QUALITY MARKER IS EITHER 1, 2 OR 3.  VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE IS CONSIDERED SUSPECT.  THIS IS VALID ONLY FOR RADIOSONDE, DROPWINSONDE, AND MULTI-LEVEL RECONNAISSANCE DATA TYPES. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE W3LIB ROUTINE GBLEVENTS. TRC-> TOB=#, TQM=3
07 B TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION IS CONVERTED TO VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE, CALCULATED FROM THE REGENERATED SPECIFIC HUMIDITY (SEE R. CODE 00 OR 01 ABOVE) AND FROM A SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE OBSERVATION THAT HAS UNDERGONE POSSIBLE COMPLEX QUALITY CONTROL AND RADIATION CORRECTIONS.  THE SPECIFIC HUMIDITY HAS A REJECTED QUALITY MARKER OTHER THAN 9 OR 15, THE PRESSURE LEVEL IS BELOW 700 MB, AND THE SENSIBLE TEMPERATURE QUALITY MARKER IS EITHER 1, 2 OR 3.  VIRTUAL TEMPERATURE IS CONSIDERED SUSPECT.  THIS IS VALID ONLY FOR RADIOSONDE, DROPWINSONDE, AND MULTI-LEVEL RECONNAISSANCE DATA TYPES. THIS IS PERFORMED IN THE PROGRAM PREPOBS_CQCBUFR. TRC-> TOB=#, TQM=3

% - PRIOR TO 12/04/2007 THE QUALITY MARKER WAS SET TO 8 IN THIS CASE.
