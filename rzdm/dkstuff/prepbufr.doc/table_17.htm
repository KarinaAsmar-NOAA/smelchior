KEY:
     IN "R.CODE" COLUMN:

R.CODE MEANING MNEMONICS AFFECTED AND NEW VALUE FOR OBS, Q.M.

07 B THE MAGNITUDE OF THE WIND INCREMENT (EITHER U- OR V-COMPONENT) (OBSERVATION - GUESS) IS GREATER THAN 12 METERS/SECOND (AND REASON CODES 01-05 ABOVE DO NOT APPLY). WRC-> WQM=13
