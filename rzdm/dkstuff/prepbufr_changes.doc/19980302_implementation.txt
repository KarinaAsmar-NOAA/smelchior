MEMORANDUM TO:  Record
FROM:           Dennis A. Keyser -- W/NMC22
Subject:        Changes to 3DVAR/OI/SSI-Data Preprocessor
                (May 1, 1997 Version --
                 AVN, FNL, CDAS DATACARD UPDATE # 7.3)

Implemented 12Z 02 March 1998:

NESDIS high-density satellite winds of the types IR cloud-drift,
water vapor imager cloud top and new-format low-density picture
triplet winds all switched on for processing and assimilation by
Global and CDAS SSI.

NESDIS conventional low-density satellite winds of the type IR
cloud-drift (including picture triplet from old-format processing)
are no longer processed or assimilated.
